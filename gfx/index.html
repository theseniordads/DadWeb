An artistic Oddessy by
Jackson Pollock

Howdee-doody my friends- if you've ever wondered who is responsible for all the visual splendour of this web site and the Senior Dads demos, then look no further. Welcome to my own private gallery! (Cue spooky "Gallery" music from "Vision on".) Feel free to look around, but please remember to take all you litter home with you!

I joined the crew as they were planning to launch their website. They were sorely needing a new artistic direction for their beacon in Cyberspace, so, having heard I was an "arty" type of person, Doddering Git asked me to join the crew over a pint of snakebite at the Rolf Harris bar at the student union. I gave the website a new graphical design angle, based around a specially designed Senior Dads logo. The guys were so impressed, they asked me to join their crew as an official member! ... And the rest is history... (Cue epic historical type music.)

Do you like this background BTW? I'm now learning how to use Photoshop, so expect to see further examples of this cropping up in future demos!!
